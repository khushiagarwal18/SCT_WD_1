About Us

At SkillCraft Technology, we are dedicated to empower individuals through skills development and providing valuable internship opportunities.

With a team of more than 100 members, we strive to deliver our services.

Founded in 2024, we have been committed to continuous upskilling, empowerment through skills development, providing hands-on experience and quality and verification. Our journey started with a mission to empower individuals through skills development. The company offers various virtual internship programs designed to provide practical knowledge and hands-on experience. By focusing on continuous upskilling and providing accessible online learning opportunities, SkillCraft Technology aims to equip participants with industry-relevant skills to thrive in their careers. The company's internships are conducted entirely online, ensuring a flexible and inclusive learning environment, and we continue to innovate and grow.
